## Projects

A selection of representative work across new construction, renovation, and historical restoration in the Hudson Valley.

*This section will be populated with project descriptions and photography. [Contact Severin](/contact/) to learn more about his experience with projects similar to yours.*

---

### Historical Barn Restoration

Restoration of a mid-19th-century timber-frame barn in Columbia County. Work included structural assessment and repair of the original bent framing, re-cladding with period-appropriate board-and-batten siding, and re-roofing. The barn was returned to agricultural use while preserving its historic character.

---

### Farmhouse Renovation, Dutchess County

Full renovation of an 1880s farmhouse, balancing modern systems and comfort with the preservation of original wide-plank floors, plaster walls, and period millwork. Scope included structural repairs, new mechanical systems, kitchen and bath updates, and exterior restoration.

---

### New Residential Construction, Ulster County

Owner's representative for a new single-family residence on a rural property. Managed design development, contractor procurement, and construction administration from site work through certificate of occupancy.
