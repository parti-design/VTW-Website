This project involved planning and construction support for a new guesthouse on an existing rural property. Work focused on early-phase decision support, contractor selection strategy, and owner-side oversight during key construction milestones.

The core objective was to keep scope and cost aligned while preserving flexibility for finish decisions later in the schedule.
