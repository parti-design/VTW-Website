## Welcome

Severin Winter brings decades of hands-on experience to every project. Based in the Hudson Valley, he works with homeowners, developers, and preservation organizations on a wide range of building projects — from ground-up construction to sensitive restorations of 18th- and 19th-century structures.

Whether you are planning a new build, undertaking a major renovation, or working to preserve a piece of local history, Severin provides practical guidance grounded in real construction knowledge.

### Areas of Expertise

- **New Construction** — residential and light commercial builds, project management, contractor coordination
- **Renovation** — structural assessments, scope development, value engineering, owner's representation
- **Historical Restoration** — barns, farmhouses, and period structures; traditional materials and methods; working with preservation boards

### Serving the Hudson Valley

Severin is based in the Hudson Valley and works throughout the region, including Columbia, Dutchess, Greene, and Ulster counties.
